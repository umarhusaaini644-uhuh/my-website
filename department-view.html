Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for heart diseases. From diagnostics to advanced surgical procedures, our team ensures world-class treatment.

  • Heart Failure Management

Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating brain, spine, and nerve-related disorders.

  • Spinal Disorder Treatment
  • Neuro Rehabilitation

Dedicated to children’s health, our Pediatric Department provides preventive and curative care for infants to teenagers.

  • Child Vaccination
  • Growth & Nutrition Monitoring
  • Specialized Child Care Units

Orthopedics

We provide advanced care for bone, joint, and muscle-related conditions, ensuring mobility and quality of life.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Fracture & Trauma Care
  • Sports Injury Treatment
